<p><p>The gravure printing ink market comprises three main types: Conventional Gravure Ink, Plastic Gravure Ink, and Alcohol Soluble Gravure Ink. Conventional gravure inks are typically used for various substrates, offering high quality and durability. Plastic gravure inks are formulated for printing on plastic materials, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. Alcohol soluble gravure inks, known for their fast drying and low odor, are widely used in packaging and labels, making them suitable for eco-friendly applications. Each type caters to specific printing needs and industries.</p></p>
