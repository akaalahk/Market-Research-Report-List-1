<p><p>Antibody labeling kits are essential tools in biomedical research and diagnostics, facilitating the identification and quantification of specific proteins. The market is segmented into two primary types: fluorescent labels and enzyme labels. Fluorescent labels utilize fluorescent dyes to enable the visualization of antibodies under UV light, providing high sensitivity and resolution. Enzyme labels, on the other hand, employ enzyme-linked reagents that catalyze colorimetric reactions, allowing for the detection of antibodies through chromogenic methods. Both types enhance the precision of immunoassays and flow cytometry applications.</p></p>
