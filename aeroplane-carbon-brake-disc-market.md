<p><p>The aeroplane carbon brake disc market is categorized into three main types: single disc brakes, dual disc brakes, and multiple disc brakes. Single disc brakes involve one brake disc per wheel, suitable for smaller aircraft with lighter braking requirements. Dual disc brakes consist of two discs per wheel, providing enhanced braking performance for larger aircraft. Multiple disc brakes feature three or more discs, offering superior heat dissipation and braking efficiency, making them ideal for high-performance and heavy aircraft applications.</p></p>
