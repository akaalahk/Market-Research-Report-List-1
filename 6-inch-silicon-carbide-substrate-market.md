<p><p>The 6-inch silicon carbide substrate market is categorized into two main types based on electrical properties: conductive and semi-insulated. In contrast, semi-insulated substrates possess reduced conductivity, providing better thermal management and enabling higher voltage applications, such as electric vehicles and renewable energy technologies. Both types cater to growing demands in advanced semiconductor devices and niche applications.</p></p>
