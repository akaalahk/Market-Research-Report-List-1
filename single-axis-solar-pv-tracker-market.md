<p><p>The Single Axis Solar PV Tracker market is divided into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal trackers are designed to rotate along a north-south axis, optimizing solar exposure throughout the day by tilting panels toward the sun as it moves across the sky. In contrast, vertical trackers rotate along an east-west axis, suitable for specific geographic locations, maximizing energy capture during peak sunlight hours. Both types enhance the efficiency of solar installations by increasing energy yield compared to fixed systems.</p></p>
