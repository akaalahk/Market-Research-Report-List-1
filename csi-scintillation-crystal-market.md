<p><p>The CsI scintillation crystal market primarily features two types: CsI(Tl) and CsI(Na). CsI(Tl) is doped with thallium and is widely used in medical imaging and nuclear physics due to its high light output and energy resolution. CsI(Na), doped with sodium, is characterized by its fast response time and is utilized in industrial applications and radiation detection. Both types are essential for converting ionizing radiation into visible light, making them valuable in various scientific and technological applications.</p></p>
