<p><p>The manually caulking gun market features two main types: closed barrel and open barrel caulking guns. Closed barrel caulking guns are designed with a sealed cartridge system, preventing caulk from drying out and enabling precise application. They are ideal for professional use and heavy-duty projects. In contrast, open barrel caulking guns have an exposed barrel where cartridges can be inserted easily, making them user-friendly and suitable for lighter tasks. Both types serve specific needs in construction and DIY applications.</p></p>
