<p><p>The Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillator (TCXO) market can be segmented into two primary types based on form factors: PIN Shape and SMD (Surface Mount Device) Shape. The PIN Shape TCXOs, characterized by their protruding pins, are typically suited for through-hole mounting in circuit boards, offering robust performance in various environments. On the other hand, SMD Shape TCXOs are designed for surface mounting, allowing for a compact design and efficient assembly processes. Both types are essential for applications demanding precise frequency stability across temperature variations.</p></p>
