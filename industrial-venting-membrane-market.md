<p><p>The Industrial Venting Membrane market encompasses two primary types: adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents are designed for easy installation, featuring a sticky surface that adheres directly to various substrates, ensuring a secure seal while allowing moisture vapor to escape. On the other hand, vents without backing material are typically used in applications requiring flexibility and adaptability, as they can conform to uneven surfaces while still providing effective ventilation. Both types are essential for maintaining proper airflow and preventing moisture buildup in industrial settings.</p></p>
