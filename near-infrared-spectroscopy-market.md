<p><p>Near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) encompasses various types, including Fourier Transform Near-Infrared (FT-NIR) and Ultraviolet-Visible Near-Infrared (UV-Vis-NIR). FT-NIR utilizes Fourier Transform techniques for rapid and precise spectral data analysis, making it suitable for complex samples. Conversely, UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light measurement with near-infrared, providing a broader spectral range for material characterization. Both methods are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and food processing for quality control, composition analysis, and process monitoring.</p></p>
