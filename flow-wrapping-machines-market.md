<p><p>Flow wrapping machines are vital in the packaging industry, classified into horizontal and vertical types based on their configuration. Horizontal flow wrapping machines operate by placing products on a conveyor and wrapping them in film horizontally, suitable for flat or bulkier items. In contrast, vertical flow wrapping machines utilize a gravity-based feeding system, ideal for smaller or lighter products, allowing for a compact design. Each type serves unique packaging needs, enhancing efficiency and presentation in various market applications.</p></p>
