<p><p>The axle counters market is categorized into two main types: with wheel and without wheel. </p><p>Axle counters with wheel involve sensors placed on the rail that detect the passage of train wheels, providing precise counting and train positioning information. </p><p>Without wheel systems use alternative technologies, such as electromagnetic or infrared sensors, to monitor train movement without direct contact with the wheels. Both systems enhance railway safety and efficiency by accurately monitoring train locations and preventing collisions.</p></p>
