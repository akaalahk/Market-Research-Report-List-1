<p><p>The biconical antennas market is categorized into two main types: omni-directional and directional antennas. Omni-directional antennas radiate signals uniformly in all directions, making them ideal for applications requiring 360-degree coverage, such as in base stations. In contrast, directional antennas focus their energy in a specific direction, enhancing signal strength and range, which is beneficial for point-to-point communication. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various communication needs in telecommunication, broadcasting, and military applications.</p></p>
