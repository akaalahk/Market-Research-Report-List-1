<p><ul><li>Equilibrium dialysis</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li><li>Ultracentrifugation</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Protein Binding Assay market is categorized into several methods for measuring drug-protein interactions. Equilibrium dialysis enables the separation of free and bound drug fractions across a semi-permeable membrane. Ultrafiltration employs pressure to separate unbound drugs from protein-bound fractions using a filter. Ultracentrifugation utilizes high-speed centrifugation to differentiate between bound and free drug based on density. Other methods may include techniques like microdialysis or chromatography, catering to specialized applications in pharmacokinetics and drug development.</p></p>
