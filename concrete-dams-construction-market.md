<p><p>The concrete dams construction market features several types, including normal concrete dams, roller compacted concrete dams, and prefabricated concrete dams. Normal concrete dams are built using traditional reinforced concrete techniques, suitable for various water storage applications. Roller compacted concrete dams utilize a dry mix that is compacted with heavy machinery, providing cost-effective solutions for rapid construction. Prefabricated concrete dams consist of pre-assembled sections transported and installed on-site, offering flexibility and reduced construction time, ideal for challenging terrain or environments.</p></p>
