<p><ul><li>In-series</li><li>Between-series</li><li>T-shaped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF adapters are essential components in wireless communication and signal transmission, categorized into three main types: in-series, between-series, and T-shaped. In-series adapters connect two components of the same type, ensuring continuity in signal flow. Between-series adapters facilitate connections between differing connector types, enhancing versatility in systems. T-shaped adapters feature a central connector with two branching paths, allowing for multiple signal routes from a single source. Each type plays a critical role in optimizing connectivity and performance in RF applications.</p></p>
