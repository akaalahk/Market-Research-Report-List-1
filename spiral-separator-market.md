<p><p>The spiral separator market encompasses two primary types: wet spiral separators and dry spiral separators. Wet spiral separators utilize water as a medium to separate materials based on density, commonly used in mineral processing and coal washing. They are effective for separating fine particles. In contrast, dry spiral separators operate without water, relying on airflow and gravity to separate heavier materials from lighter ones, ideal for dry sorting applications. Both types serve crucial roles in efficient material separation across various industries.</p></p>
