<p><strong>The Diffuse Reflective Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Diffuse Sensors</li><li>Diffuse Sensors with Background Suppression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diffuse reflective sensors are essential in various automation applications, categorized primarily into normal diffuse sensors and diffuse sensors with background suppression. Normal diffuse sensors detect objects by reflecting emitted light off surfaces, working effectively over short distances. In contrast, diffuse sensors with background suppression enhance detection accuracy by ignoring reflections from background surfaces, focusing solely on the target object. This ability reduces false triggers and improves reliability in environments with complex backgrounds, making them ideal for precise positioning and quality control tasks.</p></p>
