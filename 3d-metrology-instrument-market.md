<p><p>The 3D Metrology Instrument market encompasses three key types: Coordinate Measuring Machines (CMM), which utilize probes to measure an object's physical geometrical characteristics; Optical Digitizers and Scanners (ODS), which employ laser or structured light to capture detailed 3D data for reverse engineering and inspection; and Video Measuring Machines (VMM), which rely on cameras and advanced optics to measure dimensions of small parts with high precision. Each instrument serves distinct applications in quality control and manufacturing.</p></p>
