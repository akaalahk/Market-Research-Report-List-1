<p><p>Effusion cells are crucial components in materials science, primarily utilized for thin film deposition processes. The market is segmented into low temperature and high temperature effusion cells. Low temperature effusion cells operate efficiently at temperatures below 500°C, suitable for sensitive materials, ensuring precision without decomposition. In contrast, high temperature effusion cells function at elevated temperatures, often exceeding 1000°C, accommodating robust materials and complex alloys. Each type caters to specific industrial applications, balancing performance requirements and material characteristics for optimal film quality.</p></p>
