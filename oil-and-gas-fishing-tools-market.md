<p><p>The Oil and Gas Fishing Tools market comprises various specialized equipment essential for recovering lost or stuck items in drilling operations. Drillpipe and casing cutters are used to sever pipes for retrieval, while washover tools facilitate the removal of stuck casing. Milling tools grind away obstructions, and safety joints provide safe separation points. Internal and external catching tools are designed to retrieve objects from within or around the drill string. Other tools may include grapples and magnets for diverse fishing applications.</p></p>
