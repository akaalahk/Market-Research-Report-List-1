Single coil coolers utilize a single spiral or straight coil to circulate cooling fluid, offering a simpler design for lower flow applications. Double coil coolers, on the other hand, incorporate two coils, providing enhanced cooling efficiency for higher flow rates and demanding applications, ensuring accurate monitoring and compliance.</p></p>
