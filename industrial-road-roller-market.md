<p><strong>The Industrial Road Roller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibratory Roller</li><li>Static Road Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial road roller market primarily consists of two types: vibratory rollers and static road rollers. Vibratory rollers utilize oscillating steel drums to achieve efficient compaction through vibration, making them ideal for asphalt and granular materials in road construction. In contrast, static road rollers rely solely on their weight to compress surfaces, effectively used for compacting soil and gravel. Each type serves distinct applications, contributing to the overall efficiency and effectiveness of road-building projects.</p></p>
