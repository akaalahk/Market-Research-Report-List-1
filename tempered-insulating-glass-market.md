<p><p>The tempered insulating glass market is categorized based on interlayer gases used. Air-based interlayer gas is commonly utilized for standard applications, offering basic insulation properties. Inert gas interlayers, such as argon or krypton, enhance thermal efficiency and reduce energy loss, making them ideal for high-performance windows. Other market solutions may include specialized gases or hybrid systems that combine different technologies to optimize insulation and soundproofing. Each type caters to varying energy efficiency needs and application specifications.</p></p>
