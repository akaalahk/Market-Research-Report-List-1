<p><strong>The Melamine Board Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Melamine Faced Chipboard</li><li>Medium Density Fiberboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The melamine board market primarily includes two types: Melamine Faced Chipboard (MFC) and Medium Density Fiberboard (MDF). MFC is created by applying a decorative melamine layer over chipboard, offering durability and resistance to scratches, making it ideal for furniture and cabinetry. MDF, on the other hand, is composed of wood fibers bonded with resin, providing a smooth surface suitable for detailed applications like cabinetry and decorative moldings. Both types are popular in interior design for their aesthetic appeal and versatility.</p></p>
