<p><p>The connected street lights market comprises two primary types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems utilize Power Line Communication (PLC), leveraging existing electrical wiring for data transmission, offering reliability and reduced interference. Conversely, wireless systems employ technologies like Wi-Fi, Zigbee, or cellular networks to facilitate communication, providing flexibility in installation and scalability. Each type has its advantages; wired systems ensure consistent connectivity, while wireless options allow for easier updates and adaptability to changing urban landscapes.</p></p>
