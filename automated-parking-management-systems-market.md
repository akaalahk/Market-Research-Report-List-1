<p><p>Automated Parking Management Systems are classified into two main types: Semi-automated and Fully Automated systems. Semi-automated Parking Systems require some human intervention, where users may need to park the vehicle in designated zones while the system manages the rest, enhancing space efficiency. Conversely, Fully Automated Parking Systems operate entirely without human assistance; vehicles are parked by the system through robotic technology. Both types aim to optimize space utilization, reduce congestion, and improve the overall parking experience.</p></p>
