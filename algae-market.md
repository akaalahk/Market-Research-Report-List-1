<p><p>The algae market encompasses various cultivation techniques, including open ponds, raceway ponds, closed photobioreactors, and closed fermenter systems. Open ponds utilize natural sunlight and are cost-effective but less controlled. Raceway ponds, a type of open system, facilitate better water circulation. Closed photobioreactors offer controlled environments for higher yields and purity, while closed fermenter systems optimize growth conditions, often using organic substrates. Each method caters to different scales and product types, influencing market dynamics and applications.</p></p>
