<p><strong>The Card Personalization Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card personalization services encompass three main market types: dye sublimation, retransfer, and inkjet printing. Dye sublimation uses heat to transfer dye onto cards, offering high-quality, vibrant images and is widely used for ID cards. Retransfer printing involves printing onto a film that is then fused to the card surface, providing durability and enhanced image quality, ideal for secure applications. Inkjet printing, while less common for cards, offers flexibility and lower costs for shorter runs, suitable for customized and variable data printing.</p></p>
