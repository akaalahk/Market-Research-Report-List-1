<p><p>Cardiac marker testing encompasses various market types, each utilizing distinct methodologies for diagnosing heart conditions. Chemiluminescence employs light emission for sensitive detection of biomarkers. Immunofluorescence uses fluorescent antibodies to visualize specific proteins, enhancing accuracy. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) quantifies proteins through enzyme reactions, offering high specificity. Immunochromatography provides rapid results through a simple test strip method. Other technologies may include microfluidics or mass spectrometry, broadening diagnostic capabilities in cardiology. These diverse approaches facilitate timely and effective patient care.</p></p>
