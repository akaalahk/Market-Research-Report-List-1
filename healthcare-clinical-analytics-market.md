Predictive analytics utilizes statistical models and machine learning techniques to forecast future health outcomes, enabling proactive decision-making. Prescriptive analytics goes a step further by recommending actions based on data insights, optimizing treatment plans and resource allocation to improve overall patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
