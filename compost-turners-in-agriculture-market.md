<p><p>Compost turners are essential in agriculture for efficiently mixing and aerating organic materials to produce high-quality compost. The elevating-face style compost turner features a unique design that lifts material and flips it, promoting rapid decomposition and effective aeration. In contrast, the drum style compost turner consists of a rotating drum that mixes compost as it moves, ensuring thorough blending and aeration. Both types cater to different operational scales, enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of compost production in various agricultural settings.</p></p>
