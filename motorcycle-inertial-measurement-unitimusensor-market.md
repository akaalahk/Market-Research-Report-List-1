<p><p>The Motorcycle Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: active and passive markets. Active IMUs require power to operate and provide real-time data on motorcycle dynamics, enhancing safety and performance through features like traction control and stability management. Conversely, passive IMUs contain no internal power source and rely on external signals or passive motion sensing. While active systems offer advanced functionality, passive systems are typically more cost-effective and simpler to integrate into existing designs.</p></p>
