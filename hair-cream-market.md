<p><p>The hair cream market is categorized primarily into three types: W/O (water-in-oil), O/W (oil-in-water), and other formulations. W/O creams offer a richer, more moisturizing texture, ideal for dry or coarse hair, as they create a barrier to lock in moisture. O/W creams, lighter and more easily absorbed, are suited for oily or fine hair, providing hydration without heaviness. Other formulations may include specialty creams with added benefits, such as UV protection or styling hold, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
