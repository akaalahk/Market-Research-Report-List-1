<p><p>The Industrial Computed Tomography Equipment market is divided into two main types: stationary and portable equipment. Stationary computed tomography equipment is typically large, designed for high-volume and complex inspections in fixed locations, offering enhanced accuracy and detail for analyzing intricate components. In contrast, portable computed tomography equipment is lightweight and mobile, allowing for on-site scanning and inspection of various materials and structures. This flexibility makes it ideal for industries requiring quick assessments without the need to transport items to a dedicated facility.</p></p>
