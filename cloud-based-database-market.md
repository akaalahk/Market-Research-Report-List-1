<p><strong>The Cloud-based Database Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SQL Database</li><li>NoSQL Database</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cloud-based database market is primarily divided into two types: SQL and NoSQL databases. SQL databases, or relational databases, utilize structured query language for defined data schemas, ensuring consistency and complex querying capabilities. In contrast, NoSQL databases are designed for unstructured and semi-structured data, offering flexibility and scalability, making them ideal for handling large volumes of diverse datasets in real-time applications. Both types serve different business needs, with SQL focusing on transactional integrity and NoSQL facilitating rapid data storage and retrieval.</p></p>
