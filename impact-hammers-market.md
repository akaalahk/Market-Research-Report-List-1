<p><p>The impact hammers market is primarily divided into hydraulic and electric impact hammers. Hydraulic impact hammers utilize fluid pressure to deliver powerful, repetitive strikes, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications like pile driving and construction. They offer high energy efficiency and minimal noise. In contrast, electric impact hammers are powered by electricity, providing a cleaner, quieter alternative for lighter tasks such as demolition or precision drilling. Both types cater to distinct operational needs and preferences in construction and industrial sectors.</p></p>
