<p><strong>The Polyurethane Reactive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-sagging Adhesive</li><li>Self-leveling Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane reactive adhesive market is categorized primarily into non-sagging and self-leveling adhesives. Non-sagging adhesives are formulated to maintain their position on vertical or overhead surfaces, making them ideal for applications like construction and automotive assembly. In contrast, self-leveling adhesives are designed to spread evenly across surfaces, filling gaps and ensuring a smooth finish, often used in flooring and tiling. Both types offer strong bonds and flexibility, catering to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
