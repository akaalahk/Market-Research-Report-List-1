<p><strong>The Gear Hydraulic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>Gear Ring Pump</li><li>Screw Spindle Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gear hydraulic pump market comprises several types, including external gear pumps, internal gear pumps, gear ring pumps, and screw spindle pumps. External gear pumps use two meshing gears for fluid transfer, offering high efficiency. Internal gear pumps utilize an inner gear revolving within an outer casing, providing a smooth flow. Gear ring pumps, characterized by their unique design, enhance flow stability. Screw spindle pumps employ a helical screw mechanism, ideal for low-viscosity fluids, ensuring precision and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
