<p><strong>The Enhanced PoE Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.3af PoE</li><li>802.3at PoE +</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enhanced PoE switch market encompasses various Power over Ethernet (PoE) standards. The 802.3af PoE standard, also known as PoE, delivers up to 15.4 watts per port, suitable for devices like IP phones and basic cameras. In contrast, the 802.3at PoE+ standard provides up to 30 watts per port, catering to more power-demanding devices such as pan-tilt-zoom cameras, wireless access points, and advanced IP surveillance systems. This differentiation allows for tailored network solutions based on power needs.</p></p>
