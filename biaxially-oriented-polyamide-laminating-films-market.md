<p><p>Biaxially oriented polyamide laminating films are categorized based on the type of adhesive used in their application. Solvent-based laminating films utilize organic solvents for adhesion, providing excellent bonding strength and flexibility but may have environmental concerns. Water-based films employ water as a solvent, offering a more eco-friendly option with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Other types include hot melt adhesives and ultraviolet (UV) cure systems, each designed to meet specific performance requirements and application scenarios across various industries, including packaging and electronics.</p></p>
