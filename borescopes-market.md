<p><p>The borescopes market is divided into two main types: flexible borescopes and rigid borescopes. Flexible borescopes are designed with a flexible shaft, allowing for easier access to hard-to-reach areas, making them ideal for inspecting complex machinery and pipelines. In contrast, rigid borescopes offer a straight, inflexible design, providing high-resolution images in environments where access is direct. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing, automotive, and aviation, each catering to specific inspection needs based on orientation and accessibility.</p></p>
