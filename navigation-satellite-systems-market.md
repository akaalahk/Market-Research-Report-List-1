<p><p>The Navigation Satellite Systems market encompasses three primary types: Global Constellations, Regional Constellations, and Satellite-Based Augmentations. Global Constellations provide worldwide coverage and include systems like GPS and GLONASS. Regional Constellations focus on specific geographic areas for enhanced navigation services, such as the European Galileo system. Satellite-Based Augmentations enhance the accuracy and reliability of existing GNSS signals, offering improvements for applications like aviation and agriculture. Together, these systems support a wide range of navigation and positioning applications globally.</p></p>
