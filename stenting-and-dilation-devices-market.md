<p><p>Stenting and dilation devices are medical instruments used mainly in interventional procedures to treat various cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, and other conditions. Stents, usually made of metal or polymer, are small mesh tubes inserted into narrowed or blocked blood vessels or ducts to keep them open. Dilation devices, such as balloons, are used in procedures like angioplasty to expand these narrowed areas. These devices are crucial in managing diseases like coronary artery disease, peripheral artery disease, and more.</p><p>The stenting and dilation devices market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in technology, and the rise in minimally invasive procedures. Increasing awareness regarding the benefits of these devices, coupled with a growing geriatric population, is propelling market expansion. Furthermore, the introduction of innovative products, such as drug-eluting stents and bioresorbable stents, is set to enhance patient outcomes and drive adoption.</p><p>The stenting and dilation devices market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
