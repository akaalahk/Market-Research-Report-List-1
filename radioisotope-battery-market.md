<p><strong>The Radioisotope Battery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioisotope battery market consists of two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. Thermal conversion types utilize heat generated by radioactive decay to produce electricity, employing thermoelectric materials to convert temperature differentials into power. Conversely, non-thermal conversion types leverage direct energy from beta decay or other radioactive processes, often utilizing semiconductor technologies to generate electrical energy without relying on heat differentials. Both types serve niche applications, particularly in space and remote power systems, where long-lasting energy sources are critical.</p></p>
