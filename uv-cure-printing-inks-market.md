<p><p>UV cure printing inks are specialized inks that utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure or dry during the printing process. The market is primarily segmented into two types: Arc Curing and LED Curing. Arc Curing involves the use of high-intensity UV lamps that emit broad-spectrum light, providing quick curing and strong adhesion. In contrast, LED Curing employs energy-efficient LED lamps that emit specific wavelengths, offering longer lifespan and lower heat output, making it suitable for heat-sensitive substrates.</p></p>
