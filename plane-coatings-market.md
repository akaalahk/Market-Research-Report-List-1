<p><p>The plane coatings market primarily comprises liquid coatings and powder coatings. Liquid coatings, which include paints and varnishes, are applied in a fluid state, forming a smooth finish upon drying. They offer versatility in application and color options. Conversely, powder coatings are applied in a dry powder form and cured under heat, resulting in a durable, high-quality finish with better resistance to chipping and fading. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing aesthetics and protecting surfaces from environmental damage.</p></p>
