<p><p>The Organic Ultrafiltration Membrane Market includes several types, each catering to specific applications. Flat membranes, characterized by their planar structure, offer high surface area and efficiency. Tubular membranes have a cylindrical shape, ideal for viscous feeds and easy cleaning. Capillary membranes feature hollow fibers that provide high packing density and flexibility for various processes. Other membrane types may include spiral-wound and sheet configurations, each designed to optimize performance in diverse filtration tasks across industries like water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
