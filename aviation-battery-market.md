<p><p>The aviation battery market comprises three primary types: Nickel Cadmium (NiCd), Lead Acid, and Lithium Ion batteries. NiCd batteries are known for their robustness and ability to perform in extreme temperatures, making them suitable for older aircraft. Lead Acid batteries are heavy but cost-effective, often used for starting engines and emergency systems. Lithium Ion batteries are gaining popularity due to their lightweight, high energy density, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for modern, fuel-efficient aircraft applications.</p></p>
