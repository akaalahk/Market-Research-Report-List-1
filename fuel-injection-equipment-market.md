<p><strong>The Fuel Injection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)</li><li>Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injection equipment market comprises two main types: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection (MFI). EFI utilizes electronic controls to optimize fuel delivery, enhancing efficiency and performance in modern vehicles. It offers precise fuel atomization and real-time adjustments based on engine conditions. In contrast, MFI relies on mechanical components to inject fuel, providing simplicity and reliability, often found in older or less complex engines. Both systems play critical roles in managing fuel efficiency and emissions in automotive applications.</p></p>
