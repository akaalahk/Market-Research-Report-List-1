<p><p>The thyroid function test market comprises various types of assessments to evaluate thyroid health. TSH (Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone) tests measure the hormone that regulates thyroid gland function, serving as a primary screening tool. T4 tests assess thyroxine levels, essential for metabolism, while T3 tests measure triiodothyronine, indicating thyroid activity. Together, these tests inform diagnosis and management of thyroid conditions, catering to both clinical and home testing needs.</p></p>
