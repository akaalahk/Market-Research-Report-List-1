<p><strong>The Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vane-type VVT</li><li>Gear-type VVT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Valve Timing (VVT) systems enhance engine efficiency by adjusting the timing of valve openings and closings. The vane-type VVT utilizes a mechanism with vanes that change position in response to engine oil pressure, allowing for optimal valve timing across various RPMs. On the other hand, gear-type VVT employs a gear system to alter the valve timing, providing precise adjustments. Both types are integral to modern engines, improving performance, fuel efficiency, and reducing emissions in the automotive market.</p></p>
