<p><p>The piezoceramics market is primarily categorized into several types based on composition and properties. Lead Zinc Titanates (PZT) are widely used for their high piezoelectric coefficients, making them ideal for sensors and actuators. Lead Titanate (PT) offers excellent ferroelectric properties, suitable for ultrasonics and transducers. Lead Magnesium Niobate (PMN) features exceptional dielectric properties and is often utilized in high-performance applications. Other types include various non-lead based ceramics and composite materials, catering to specific industry needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
