<p><strong>The Aluminum Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alumina</li><li>Primary Aluminum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum market comprises alumina and primary aluminum. Alumina, or aluminum oxide, is derived from bauxite ore through refining and serves as the raw material for aluminum production. It is traded globally, with prices influenced by supply-demand dynamics and energy costs. Primary aluminum is produced by smelting alumina using electrolysis, forming pure aluminum metal. Its market is driven by industrial demand, particularly in construction, automotive, and packaging sectors. Both markets are interconnected, with alumina being a critical input for primary aluminum production.</p></p>
