<p><p>The soft ferrite core market consists of various types, notably Nickel-Zinc, Manganese-Zinc, and Magnesium-Zinc ferrite cores. Manganese-Zinc ferrite cores offer higher magnetic permeability, making them suitable for low-frequency applications like transformers and inductors.
